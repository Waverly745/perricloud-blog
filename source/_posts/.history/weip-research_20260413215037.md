## 科研成果 / Research Achievements

**研究方向 / Research Direction:**  
基于忆阻器的稀疏神经网络芯片关键技术研究  
Research on Key Technologies of Memristor-Based Sparse Neural Network Chips

### 2025

**A Bit Level Weight Reordering Strategy Based on Column Similarity to Explore Weight Sparsity in RRAM-based NN Accelerator**  
- 设计稀疏神经网络加速器，利用 bit 级别的重排序算法降低计算和存储开销  
  Designed a sparse neural network accelerator and proposed a bit-level weight reordering strategy to reduce computation and storage overhead.
- **International Conference on Parallel and Distributed Systems (ICPADS 2025)**，录用  
  Accepted by **International Conference on Parallel and Distributed Systems (ICPADS 2025)**

**Segmented Exponent Alignment and Dynamic Wordline Activation for Floating-Point Analog CIM Macros**  
- 利用分段指数对齐与动态字线激活技术解决浮点存算宏中的高延迟等问题  
  Proposed segmented exponent alignment and dynamic wordline activation to address high latency and related issues in floating-point analog CIM macros.
- **International Conference on Electronics Circuits and Systems (ICECS 2025)**，录用  
  Accepted by **International Conference on Electronics Circuits and Systems (ICECS 2025)**

### 2024

**An Integration and Time-Sampling based Readout Circuit with Current Compensation for Parallel MAC Operations in RRAM Arrays**  
- 基于 28nm 工艺设计，对忆阻器阵列并行乘累加电流利用时间采样实现量化读出  
  Designed in 28 nm CMOS, this work proposes a time-sampling-based readout circuit with current compensation for quantized readout of parallel MAC currents in RRAM arrays.
- **IEEE International Symposium on Circuits and Systems (ISCAS 2024)**，发表  
  Published at **IEEE International Symposium on Circuits and Systems (ISCAS 2024)**

**An Energy-Efficient Reconfigurable 18/12-Bit 1MS/s Pipelined-SAR ADC**  
- 基于 180nm 工艺设计，1 MS/s 采样率、18/12-bit 可重构低功耗 SAR ADC  
  Designed in 180 nm CMOS, this work presents an energy-efficient reconfigurable 18/12-bit 1 MS/s pipelined-SAR ADC.
- **AEU - International Journal of Electronics and Communications**，发表  
  Published in **AEU - International Journal of Electronics and Communications**

- **华中赛区三等奖**  
  **Third Prize, Central China Regional Competition**

### 2023

**第 7 届集成电路创新创业大赛“芯海杯” / The 7th Integrated Circuit Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition (“Chipsea Cup”)**  
**18位高精度 SAR ADC 设计 / 18-bit High-Precision SAR ADC Design**  
- 队长  
  Team Leader
- 采用 12/6 bit 两级设计，CDAC 作为开关电容器重复使用以放大残差并节省面积，采用动态逻辑设计。180 nm 工艺下，在 1 MS/s 时功耗仅为 1.76 mW。  
  Proposed a two-stage 12/6-bit architecture. The CDAC was reused as switched capacitors to amplify the residue and save area, with dynamic logic design adopted. Implemented in 180 nm CMOS, the power consumption was only 1.76 mW at 1 MS/s.
- **华中赛区一等奖、国家三等奖**  
  **First Prize in the Central China Regional Competition, Third Prize at the National Level**
